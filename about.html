I've gained professional experience with organizations such as Rapidev, Periegesis, Voltquant, and VisionTech360, and have delivered more than 80 freelance projects worldwide through platforms like Fiverr and Upwork. Each experience has added new skills and perspectives, shaping the way I approach geospatial problems today.
